# Hub System Map

This is the clean operating split for Kosatiks Group.

## 1. Client-facing systems
These are the places that can lead to paid work, delivery, or scoped collaboration.

- `K Venture AI Studio`
- `Data Specialist Portfolio`
- `SEO Specialist Portfolio`

Optional later:
- selected audits or custom systems under `K-RnD Hub`, but only when they are packaged clearly

## 2. Signature and proof systems
These are your strongest proof-of-work layers. They build authority and public trust.

- `Kosatiks Group`
- `K-RnD Hub`
- `Master Prep 2026`
- `IT Market Monitor`
- `ACTINSPACE Zorepad`

## 3. Self systems
These are not primarily for clients. They feed your philosophy, publishing, and internal continuity.

- `K Life OS`
- `K Publishing & Library`
- `K Mentorship Hub` as a light beta, not a full community machine

## 4. Recommended naming logic
Use one consistent family:

- `K Venture AI Studio` = build and service layer
- `K-RnD Hub` = research and proof layer
- `K Life OS` = self-system layer
- `K Publishing & Library` = books, notes, essays, reading layer
- `K Mentorship Hub` = selective beta layer

## 5. What to ship now
Ship now:
- `Kosatiks Group`
- `K Venture AI Studio`
- `K-RnD Hub`
- `K Life OS`

Keep lightweight / beta:
- `K Mentorship Hub`
- `K Publishing & Library`

Do not overbuild now:
- Discord-first community
- complex cohort mechanics
- talent pool platform
- academy infrastructure

## 6. Practical rule
If a hub requires constant moderation, constant posting, and constant emotional labor, do not make it core right now.
If a hub can be maintained through pages, links, case studies, and periodic updates, it is a good fit for the current stage.
