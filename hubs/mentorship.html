Current status

ParkedBetaNot Discord-first

This hub should not be a giant Discord + parser + talent-pool machine yet. It should become a clean, understandable beta product first.

What this hub is for

  • test mentorship positioning
  • collect early interest
  • show the concept clearly

What belongs here

  • one beta landing page
  • one waitlist or intake
  • simple explanation of paths and value

What not to force yet

  • full community operations
  • many parallel forms
  • premature automation and moderation work

Recommended route

First make a separate lightweight repo and landing page. Then connect one intake database in Notion. Only later add community and automation layers if real demand appears.
